# Globe Secrets
There are many globes which can only be obtained by renaming one in an anvil.

The following is a list of the presently existing *secret* globes.

- **Agrona** - Pancake world. 
- **Flat** - Flat Earth.
- **Sun** - The Sun.
- **Moon** - The Moon
- **Artkek** - Earth but black and gold.
- **Arzinist** - The World Screams.
- **Dausora** - Earth but brown and beige.
- **E_Y_E_** - Earth but black and red.
- **FishSupreme** - Bingus.
- **Wais** - Snowglobe.
- **Zam** - Deathstar.
- **Leftchaotix** - Star world.
- **Little_Pianist** - Frozen Steve.
- **Looxond** - Some other world.
- **Tofanelly** - Toffanelly's face.
- **ThugPug43** - Cryptic shapes.
- **SylveticHearts** - Trans planet.
- **PlantKillable** - Plantkillable's face.

Additionally, if you shear a globe, it will cut a slice out of it.
