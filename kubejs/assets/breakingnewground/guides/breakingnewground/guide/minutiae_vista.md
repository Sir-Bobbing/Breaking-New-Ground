# Vista 
Vista™ Brand home entertainment systems, even today are still operational despite the total loss of infrastructure. 

A true marvel of engineering.

---
## Television
Vista is a modest mod, having two main features, Televisions <ItemImage id="vista:television" scale="0.5"/> and Viewfinders <ItemImage id="vista:viewfinder" scale="0.5"/>

Televisions can be crafted with the following recipe. However it is useless without Cassettes.
 <Recipe id="vista:television" />
 Cassettes <ItemImage id="vista:cassette" scale="0.5"/> can be obtained by having a Pillager kill a Creeper[.](secret_cassettes.md) 
 
 Or rarely from chest loot.

You can insert any cassette into any activated Television and have it play the cassette.

---
## Viewfinder
The second feature is the Viewfinder. being crafted as follows.
<Recipe id="vista:viewfinder" />
The Viewfinder on its own behaves as a placeable spyglass, allowing for you to look around.

Some other features it includes is being able to place a mob head on its lens to change the filter when looking through the Viewfinder. and having the ability to have the Viewfinder's position locked with <KeyBind id="key.attack" />.

---
## Hollow Cassette
Now the Television and Viewfinder can be used together via the Hollow Cassette <ItemImage id="vista:hollow_cassette" scale="0.5"/>.
<Recipe id="vista:hollow_cassette" />

The Hollow Cassette when <KeyBind id="key.use" /> is used on a Viewfinder, will cause the Cassette to be linked to the Viewfinder. And when inserted into a Television, will **Display** the viewfinder's perspective.

An incredibly useful feature for creating cameras and surveying areas you can't see.
