# Denizens of the world
The Overworld has been home to many creatures and civilizations. At the present there are two known races and one sub-race

---
## Villagers

Known as Testificates in some parts. The villagers are one of the oldest still surviving races in the Overworld. consisting of several cultures, with a notable subrace known as the 'Illagers'.

Villagers themselves are pacifists to a fault, but are willing to trade. With their primary currency being **Emeralds**  <ItemImage id="minecraft:emerald" scale="0.5"/>.

<GameScene zoom={4} interactive={true} float={left}>    <Entity id="minecraft:villager"/></GameScene>
##### Locations
Villagers may be found in many places of the Overworld. Their most common settling points are:
- Deserts
- Savannas
- Plains
- Taigas
- Snowy Plains

##### Trading
Being the most formalized, trading with villagers actually lets you choose what item you want from them.
Each villager can sell different items based on their profession.

*Tip: Villagers can be lured with the prospect of a good deal by holding an **Emerald Block** <ItemImage id="minecraft:emerald_block" scale="0.5"/>.*

---
## Kobolds

Kobolds dwell underneath the Overworld's surface. Having survived due to their craftiness and overall secluded nature.

They can be pet with <KeyBind id="key.use" />, and will attack if provoked.
<GameScene zoom={4} interactive={true} float={left}>    <Entity id="kobolds:kobold"/></GameScene>

*Tip: can be bred with Amethyst*
##### Locations
Kobolds can be found anywhere in the overworld underground.
##### Bartering
Dealing with the Kobolds isn't as formal as the Testifcates, giving you only what they seem as fair.
You can either drop an **Emerald** for them to pick up, or be civilized and use <KeyBind id="key.use" /> to give it to them.

After a few seconds of admiring the emerald, they'll toss you something in return,
mostly some stones and occasionally tools.
