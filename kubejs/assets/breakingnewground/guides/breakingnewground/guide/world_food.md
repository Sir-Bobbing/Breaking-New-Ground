# Food & Nutrition
Nutrition and a balanced diet is vital if you want to survive, and even prosper in the world.

---
## Spice of Life 
The Spice of Life make it that you must eat a variety of foods. If you do, you'll have stats and values increase with your variety.

Each food has a 'Diversity' value, and eating it too consistently will make that value go down, and its benefits diminish. 

To help you not flood your inventory with a plethora of food items because you need to keep a balanced diet. You can craft the **Lunchbag** <ItemImage id='solonion:lunchbag' scale="0.5"/>

The Lunch bag can also be upgraded to an an iron and even golden lunch box. each with more room for food.

## Farmer's Delight

A large variety of meals and even feasts exist. In order to make these foods, 

it'd be good to know where you can find the ingredients to craft them.


##### Beaches:
- <ItemImage id="farmersdelight:wild_cabbages" scale="0.5" /> Wild Cabbage: A large bush with small yellow flowers;
- <ItemImage id="farmersdelight:wild_beetroots" scale="0.5" /> Sea Beet: A type of beet with much larger leaves;

##### Cold climates:
- <ItemImage id="farmersdelight:wild_potatoes" scale="0.5" /> Wild Potato: Similar to normal Potato patches, but with large flowers;

##### Temperate climates:
- <ItemImage id="farmersdelight:wild_onions" scale="0.5" /> Wild Onion: A thick cluster of Allium flowers usually has an onion bulb under them;
- <ItemImage id="farmersdelight:wild_carrots" scale="0.5" /> Wild Carrot: A flowering weed, with a conspicuous beige-colored base;

##### Arid climates
- <ItemImage id="farmersdelight:wild_tomatoes" scale="0.5" /> Tomato Vines: Non-spiked floor vines covered in tiny tomatoes.

##### Swamps and Jungles
- <ItemImage id="farmersdelight:wild_rice" scale="0.5" /> Wild Rice: A thinner form of cultivated rice. Thrives in shallow puddles of water.
---
*Tip: If you wish to propagate the wild varieties of these plants, you can bonemeal them with a chance they'll spread to neighboring blocks*
