##### Rotation & Stress
The entirety of Create operates on RPM and Stress.
the RPM is the speed your network runs at. Stress Units (SU) is how much you can run on your network.

Running machines at a higher RPM makes it consume more SU. Mouse over the machine in your inventory while wearing goggles to see how much exactly.
