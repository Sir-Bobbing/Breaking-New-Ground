# The Arcane
Magic, Arcana, Thaumic, Mana, Sorcery. All different names, same concept. a fundamental element of reality unseen, the fullness of its knowledge yet to be revealed and unfurled.

It is for this lack of fullness, that the Reclaimer's Manual and the PONDER system is lacking much information relating to it.
