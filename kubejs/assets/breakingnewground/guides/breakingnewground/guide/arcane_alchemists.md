# The Alchemists of Yore

The Reclaimer's Manual presently is most missing information on the 'The First Alchemists'

Not much can be said of The Alchemists, other than that they were an incredibly ancient group of people. Whom where greatly secretive with their craft. Leading to the inability to recreate their works, even today.

The only things you need to know is that their ruins still dot the land and hide underground.

## Warding Effigies
The item you'll probably interact with the **Warding Effigy** <ItemImage id="nomansland:warding_effigy"scale="0.5" />.

It is a curious trinket, that when placed down will ward off any hostile mobs in an area. The more placed in a single block the larger the area of effect.
- 1 → 24 blocks
- 2 → 44 blocks
- 3 → 60 blocks
- 4 → 72 blocks
