# Enchanting
The practice of magically enhancing one's tools and equipment is an ancient one. Dating back thousands of years.

The information behind making the enchantments has long since been lost. Only able to be copied and used with the use of **Ancient Books**  <ItemImage id="immersiveenchanting:ancient_book" scale="0.5"/>  scattered throughout the World.

---
## The Enchanting Setup
The Enchantment Table is not how you remember it. In order to get the enchantments you want on your equipment. You need to *find* the enchantment book.

---
## Minor Notes
Enchantments no longer cost experience, but instead you must find materials in order to enchant. The enchantment table will show you what materials you need for each enchant.

The ancient books can be replicated into the same book. or transmuted into a different random book

A final note, is that Mending no longer exists, but fortunately anvils no longer have any stacking costs and you can repair without worry.
