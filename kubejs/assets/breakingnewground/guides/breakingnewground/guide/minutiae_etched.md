# Etched
During the height of civilization. Etched™ Recording Services was one of the biggest around. providing the all-in-one stop for any record maker's needs.

----
## Etched Discs
Etched discs are custom Music discs that can play almost any music of your choosing. You just need to find the right format for it. Currently supported formats are:
- **MP3**
- **WAV**
- **OGG**
- **Souncloud** (minus GO+)
- **Bandcamp**
- **Minecraft Sound Events**
##### Blank Discs
Obtainable by erasing the recordings of an old disc under heat. the **Blank Disc** <ItemImage id="etched:blank_music_disc" scale="0.5"/> is the foundation of Etched. Without it, not much else would exist
<Recipe id="etched:blank_music_disc" /> 

But in order to use it, you'll need and **Etching Table** <ItemImage id="etched:etching_table" scale="0.5"/>
<Recipe id="etched:etching_table" /> 
At the top of the Etching Table UI is where you place the link to your music file. the options on the bottom are to customize pattern of the **music label** <ItemImage id="etched:music_label" scale="0.5"/> in the center.

*Tip: the link must be valid that the server can download from. your computer or discord is not valid.*
*TipTip: Etched discs can be reedited in the Etching Table.*

##### Music Labels 
Music labels can be dyed in a crafting table. Crafting two dyed music labels creates a **Complex Music Label*.

## Albums
##### Album Cover
The **Album Cover** <ItemImage id="etched:album_cover" scale="0.5"/> can hold Nine Music Discs each. and if it can detect a valid album art. The Cover try to copy the art.
Storing and extracting discs operates similar to the bundle.

##### Album Jukebox
Similar to a Jukebox but can store up to nine discs or albums. The jukebox will cycle through the songs and play them on repeat, unless powered by redstone.
