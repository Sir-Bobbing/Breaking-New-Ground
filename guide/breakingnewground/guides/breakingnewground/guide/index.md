# The Reclaimer's Manual
Greetings Reclaimer! If you are able to read this. That means you're cognizant enough to assist in the 
**Restoration** of the Overworld!

Things have changed in the Eons, long enough that the last past has been forgotten. Even you may not be able to know where you're from or who you are fully

However the past is the past and the present is **Now**. And what that means is you must explore, build and construct. For the Overworld is a garden anew for us to tend to its grounds.

As always, if you're lost Reclaimer, the Manual has most of the information you will need. If that's not enough.  Remember to use your Encyclopedic Master Interface (**E.M.I**)
