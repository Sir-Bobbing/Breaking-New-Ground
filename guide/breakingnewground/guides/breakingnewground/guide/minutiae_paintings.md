# Paintings
One of the hearts and soul of a civilization, its art. 

----
## Gallery
Paintings can now be <KeyBind id="key.use" />ed to open an interface. This interface can allow you to select any available painting in the game.

*Tip: Using shears on a painting will drop that specific painting.*
## Immersive Paintings
##### Painting
You can also make and place your own paintings using the Immersive Paintings system.

You start by crafting an Immersive **Painting** <ItemImage id="immersive_paintings:painting" scale="0.5"/> with the following. You can also craft a glowing variant
<Row>
<Recipe id="immersive_paintings:painting" /> <Recipe id="immersive_paintings:glow_painting" />
</Row>
Placing it down will create blank painting that will need to be interacted with. Once interacted you can choose from any preexisting painting, or upload your own.

##### Graffiti
Much the same as Paintings, but allows for transparency and sits flush to its surface.
