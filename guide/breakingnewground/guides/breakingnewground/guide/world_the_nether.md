# The Nether
That dark place beneath the world. hidden beneath the rock and layers and held at distance with a void between.
**The Nether** is a hostile and dangerous place for the unprepared to explore

---
## Getting in
In order to get to the nether. you need to dig to the bottom of the world. Where you will be met by a floor of obsidian.

Digging through this final layer will expose you to the void. Jumping into the void will send you into the Nether.
Now Before you jump into the Nether, you **WILL** need a **Parachute** <ItemImage id="spelunkery:parachute" scale="0.5"/> in order to survive the fall .

The parachute will automatically activate once you fall a certain distance and will slow your fall to a safe speed into the Nether.

 <Recipe id="spelunkery:parachute" />
---
## Getting out
In order to get back to the Overworld. You simply pillar back out where you fell down. Doing this will also make it that you'll no longer need a parachute to survive the fall, as you'll land on a platform of your own making.

Also note, the Nether and Overworld coordinates are One to One!

---
*Tip: Mark where you fell with a short pillar so you know where you came down*
